# Milestone Archive: v1.3 Tax + Organization Billing

**Status:** SHIPPED 2026-04-17  
**Phases:** 18–22  
**Total plans:** 23 (Phase 18: 4, 19: 5, 20: 6, 21: 6, 22: 2)

## Milestone goal

Let Phoenix SaaS teams bill organizations with Stripe Tax enabled, preserve tenant boundaries through Sigra or equivalent host-owned scopes, and hand finance workflows to Stripe-native reporting without Accrue owning accounting semantics.

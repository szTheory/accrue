# Milestone v1.9: Observability & operator runbooks

**Status:** SHIPPED 2026-04-22

**Total plans:** 8 (3 + 3 + 2)

## Overview

Close the adoption gap between “telemetry exists” and “operators know what to subscribe to, alert on, and do next” — **without** new billing primitives or Stripe Dashboard parity. Delivers an authoritative **`[:accrue, :ops, :*]`** catalog in `guides/telemetry.md`, **firehose vs ops** documentation reconciled with **`.planning/research/v1.9-TELEMETRY-GAP-AUDIT.md`**, **`Accrue.Telemetry.Metrics.defaults/0`** parity (or documented omissions) plus a **cross-domain** host subscription example, and **`guides/operator-runbooks.md`** linked from telemetry docs (**RUN-01**). **PROC-08** and **FIN-03** remain out of scope.
